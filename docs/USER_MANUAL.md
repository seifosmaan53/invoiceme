## Getting Started

### First Login

1. Open the InvoiceMe app
2. Enter your email and password
3. Tap **Login**
4. You'll be taken to the Dashboard

### Navigation

The app has 4 main sections accessible from the bottom navigation:

- **Dashboard** 📊 - Overview of your business
- **Invoices** 📄 - All your invoices
- **Clients** 👥 - Your customer database
- **Settings** ⚙️ - App configuration

## Managing Clients

### Adding a New Client

1. Go to **Clients** tab
2. Tap the **+** button (top right)
3. Fill in client information:
   - **Name** (required)
   - **Email** (optional)
   - **Phone** (optional)
   - **Address** (optional)
   - **Notes** (optional) - Add any special notes about this client
   - **Tags** (optional) - Add tags like "VIP", "Wholesale" to organize clients
4. Tap **Create Client**

### Editing a Client

1. Go to **Clients** tab
2. Tap on any client
3. Tap **Edit** (top right)
4. Make your changes
5. Tap **Save Changes**

### Searching Clients

- Use the search bar at the top of the Clients screen
- Search by name, email, phone, notes, or tags
- Results update as you type

### Client Tags

Tags help you organize and filter clients:

- Add tags when creating/editing a client
- Type a tag name and tap **Add**
- Remove tags by tapping the **X** on the tag chip
- Example tags: "VIP", "Wholesale", "Retail", "International"

## Creating Invoices

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. Go to **Invoices** tab
2. Tap the **+** button (top right)
3. Select a **Client** (required)
4. Choose **Invoice Type**:
   - **Invoice** - Standard invoice
   - **Estimate** - Quote/estimate
5. Set **Issue Date** (defaults to today)
6. Set **Due Date** (optional)
7. Choose **Currency** (default: USD)
8. **Add Items**:
   - Tap **Add Item**
   - Enter description, quantity, unit price
   - Add tax rate and discount if needed
   - Tap **Duplicate** to copy an item
   - Tap **Remove** to delete an item
9. Add **Notes** (optional)
10. Tap **Preview** (eye icon) to see how it looks
11. Tap **Create Invoice**

### Invoice Preview

Before saving, you can preview your invoice:

1. Fill in invoice details
2. Tap the **Preview** icon (👁️) in the top right
3. Review the invoice layout
4. Close preview to make changes
5. Create invoice when ready

### Invoice Items

Each invoice item includes:

- **Description** - What you're billing for
- **Quantity** - How many units
- **Unit Price** - Price per unit
- **Tax Rate** - Percentage (e.g., 10 for 10%)
- **Discount** - Percentage discount

Totals are calculated automatically:
- Line Total = Quantity × Unit Price
- After Discount = Line Total - Discount
- Tax = After Discount × Tax Rate
- Final Total = After Discount + Tax

## Managing Invoices

### Viewing Invoices

1. Go to **Invoices** tab
2. Tap any invoice to view details
3. See full invoice information, items, and totals

### Quick Actions (Swipe)

On the invoice list, swipe left on any invoice to reveal actions:

- **Edit** (blue) - Edit the invoice
- **Share** (green) - Share invoice via email, WhatsApp, etc.
- **Archive** (red) - Archive/delete the invoice

### Editing Invoices

1. Open an invoice
2. Tap **Edit** (or swipe and tap Edit)
3. Make your changes
4. Tap **Save Changes**

**Note:** Only **Draft** invoices can be edited. Once sent, you'll need to create a new invoice.

### Duplicating Invoices

Save time by duplicating existing invoices:

1. Open an invoice
2. Tap the **⋮** menu (top right)
3. Select **Duplicate Invoice**
4. A new draft invoice is created with the same details
5. Edit as needed and save

### Invoice Status

Invoices can have these statuses:

- **DRAFT** - Not yet sent
- **SENT** - Sent to client
- **PAID** - Payment received
- **OVERDUE** - Past due date, not paid
- **CANCELLED** - Cancelled invoice

### Searching Invoices

- Use the search bar at the top
- Search by invoice number or client name
- Results update as you type

### Filtering Invoices

Tap the filter icon (top right) to filter by:

- **All** - Show all invoices
- **Invoices** - Only invoices (not estimates)
- **Estimates** - Only estimates

### Sharing Invoices

1. Open an invoice
2. Tap **Share** icon (top right) or swipe and tap Share
3. Choose how to share:
   - Email
   - WhatsApp
   - AirDrop (iOS)
   - SMS
   - Other apps
4. A PDF is generated and shared

### Archiving Invoices

To remove an invoice from the main list:

1. Swipe left on the invoice
2. Tap **Archive** (red)
3. Confirm archiving

Archived invoices are soft-deleted and can be restored if needed.

## Tips & Tricks

### 💡 Productivity Tips

1. **Use Tags** - Tag clients to quickly filter (e.g., "VIP", "Monthly")
2. **Duplicate Invoices** - For recurring clients, duplicate previous invoices
3. **Preview Before Sending** - Always preview to catch errors
4. **Search Everything** - Use search to quickly find clients or invoices
5. **Swipe Actions** - Swipe left on invoices for quick edit/share/archive

### 🔄 Syncing Across Devices

- All data syncs automatically when online
- Changes made on one device appear on all devices
- Works offline - changes sync when you reconnect

### 📱 Offline Mode

- View clients and invoices offline
- Create and edit invoices offline
- Changes sync automatically when online

### 🎨 Customization

- Invoice PDFs can be customized with your logo and colors (coming soon)
- Settings allow you to configure default currency, date formats, etc.

## Need Help?

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I edit an invoice after sending it?**  
A: Only draft invoices can be edited. For sent invoices, create a new invoice or duplicate the original.

**Q: How do I mark an invoice as paid?**  
A: Open the invoice and tap "Pay Invoice" to record payment.

**Q: Can I export my invoices?**  
A: Yes! Share any invoice to export as PDF, or use the share feature.

**Q: What happens if I'm offline?**  
A: You can still view and create invoices. Changes sync when you're back online.

**Q: How do I delete a client?**  
A: Clients are archived (not permanently deleted). Contact support if you need to permanently remove data.
